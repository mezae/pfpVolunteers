Admissions Committee Contact Form

The Student Admissions Committee assists the Admissions staff in conducting certain critical aspects of the admissions process which include Parent Meetings and First Level Testing. All members have the responsibility of completing specific assignments assigned by the Admissions staff during those allotted times. The dates for these events are on Saturdays and a few on holiday breaks. We do not ask the Admissions Committee to come in during the school week.
